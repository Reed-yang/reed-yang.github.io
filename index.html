About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhengzhong Tu. I received the B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in 2025.

    • Research Interests

    My research focuses on generative video models, especially human-centric video generation and editing: controllable generation methods for motion control, identity-consistent subject synthesis, and camera reframing. I'm also interested in physics-informed priors to improve temporal coherence, realism, and controllability. I have experience in 3D/4D reconstruction and point-cloud perception.
